About Us


Leona Chemicals is a professionally managed family-owned concern.

A leaf from our book is the Sourcing Activities.
We source fine chemicals, specialty chemicals, solvents and intermediates , for end-use in the entire spectrum of manufacturing, including petrochemicals, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, paints & coatings, and related industries.

All products come from our strong, reliable, technologically sound overseas principals,
backed by our prompt, efficient and courteous services.
Quality is our major concern and we have been putting extra efforts in maintaining this objective.

While acting as marketing agent for overseas suppliers, we at Leona Chemicals are also engaged in rendering services to overseas companies for their procurement of intermediates and herbal extracts from Indian producers.
